Shri Narayani Nidhi Limited - (SNNL - Dhanam) Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Shri Narayani Nidhi Limited(SNNL Dhanam) collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of the Shri Narayani Nidhi Limited (SNNL Dhanam)("App").

Information We Collect

  • Device Information: We collect basic information about your device, such as Device ID and application ID, to ensure the App functions properly and securely.
  • Encrypted User Identification: We collect your mobile phone number and a unique user ID, both securely encrypted using the latest AES algorithm, for user identification purposes only. We do not store your phone number or user ID in plain text.

We do not collect any information about your financial accounts, balances, or transactions through the App. Your account information is retrieved directly from Shri Narayani Nidhi Limited's secure servers.

Use of Information

  • We use the collected Device Information & customer's Mobile number to maintain the App's functionality and improve your user experience.
  • We use the encrypted User Identification( such as Id & Mobile number linked with Accounts ) to link your device to your account information and personalize your experience within the App.

We will never sell or share your information with any third-party for marketing purposes.

Android Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

  • Storage Permission: This permission is used to allow you to save account information documents (like PDFs) to your device's storage. We do not access any other data on your device.
  • Storage Permission: This permission is required to enable saving documents to your device.

Security

Shri Narayani Nidhi Limited takes the security of your information very seriously. All data retrieved and sent to our servers is securely encrypted using the latest AES algorithm. We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Data Retention

We will retain your encrypted User Identification information for as long as your Shri Narayani Nidhi Limited account remains active. Once your account is closed , we will delete your encrypted User Identification information in accordance with our data retention policies.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on the App. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
